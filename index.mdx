# Welcome to Payvia

Payvia (powered by Deets) is a modern payment platform that streamlines merchant onboarding, ensures compliance, and enhances payment security. Integrate seamless, secure, and flexible payments into your software with minimal effort.

<Callout type="info" title="Why Choose Payvia?">
- Fast, secure, and PCI-compliant payment processing
- Effortless merchant onboarding
- Flexible integration for online, in-person, and subscription payments
- Advanced fraud prevention and tokenization
</Callout>

---

## How It Works (High-Level)

1. **Integrate the Deets iFrame** into your checkout to securely capture payment details.
2. **Tokenize and process payments** without sensitive data ever touching your servers.
3. **Manage payments, subscriptions, and more** via our robust API.
