## Onboarding Merchants

1. **Invite merchants** via referral link to create their merchant accounts.
2. Merchants can instantly start processing payments and withdraw earnings.
3. You earn commissions on every sale made by your merchants.

---

## Secure Transactions

1. Customers enter payment details through the Deets embedded iFrame.
2. The iFrame tokenizes and securely stores card details, ensuring compliance.
3. Transactions are processed securely, reducing fraud risk.

---

## Seamless Payment Processing

- Deets encrypts and transmits tokenized card details to the backend.
- AI-driven fraud detection analyzes transactions in real time.
- Transactions are authorized and settled, ensuring a smooth payment experience.
